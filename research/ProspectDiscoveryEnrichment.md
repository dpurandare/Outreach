# Prospect Discovery & Enrichment with Sales Navigator + Apollo.io

## Overview
This document outlines a best-practice workflow for using LinkedIn Sales Navigator and Apollo.io together to discover, enrich, and operationalize high-quality B2B prospect lists. This approach is designed for precision, not volume, and ensures your CRM remains the single source of truth.

---

## 1. Prospect Discovery (Sales Navigator)
- Use Sales Navigator to filter and identify target accounts based on:
  - Industry (e.g., Utilities, Renewables, Energy Trading)
  - Company size (e.g., 500â€“10,000 employees)
  - Geography (focus markets)
  - Recent leadership changes (high intent)
- Export the list of target accounts from Sales Navigator.

## 2. Contact Enrichment (Apollo.io)
- Import or export the Sales Navigator account list into Apollo.io.
- Use Apollo.io to:
  - Find verified emails for contacts at those accounts
  - Gather job tenure and tech stack hints
  - Enrich contact records with direct emails and phone numbers

## 3. Data Flow
- Push enriched data from Apollo.io into Zoho CRM as:
  - Account
  - Contact
  - Hero Bet Tag (for segmentation and tracking)

## 4. Workflow Summary
- **Sales Navigator:** Account-level targeting and discovery
- **Apollo.io:** Contact-level enrichment and email verification
- **Zoho CRM:** Master list, deduplication, scoring, and outreach orchestration

## 5. Why This Combo Works
- Sales Navigator provides precise B2B targeting and up-to-date org charts
- Apollo.io fills in the gaps with verified contact info and enrichment
- Combined, you get high-precision, high-quality prospect lists ready for outreach

## 6. Example Workflow
1. Filter and export accounts from Sales Navigator
2. Import accounts into Apollo.io and enrich with contact details
3. Push enriched contacts into Zoho CRM
4. Score, segment, and orchestrate outreach from CRM

---

*This workflow ensures you start with the right companies (Sales Navigator) and reach the right people with valid contact info (Apollo.io), all while keeping your CRM as the single source of truth.*
